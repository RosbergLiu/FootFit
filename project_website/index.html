Footfit

Project wonderland is to create an in-shoe pedobarography foot pressure solution

FootFit enable you to acquire pressure data using a pair of shoe pads with a sensor grid, and provide various analytical results of your gait and foot pressure distribution heatmaps.

Motivation

Motivation

For years, people suffer from leg or ankle injuries typically go through long term walk rehabilitation. During this vital process of recovery, in order to optimize the rehab schedule and validate fitness, status like natural behavior and stimulus reaction of individual patient, should be closely monitored. However, it’s currently mainly achieved by visual observation from nurse or health care stuff; the data is not quantified, hence not accurate, and, the whole process is less achievable while care personnel are busy keeping patient from staggering or even falling down.
Here we try to automate this process by monitoring one key aspect of feedback during walk rehab: foot pressure. Different pressure distribution reveals different gaits, giving medics a clear understanding of patient’s current status. However, one typical drawback of these foot pressure mapping equipment is that they are mostly stationary platform, result in inconvenience and more importantly, unnatural gait. Alternatively, an in-shoe solution provides an advantage as it reduces the risk of patients targeting the platform when walking.
